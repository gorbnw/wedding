logistics

When

August 5th, 2023

4pm - Cocktail Hour
6pm - Dinner
830ish - Ceremony
9pm - Party forever

Where

6335 51st Ave South
Seattle, WA 98118

Wedding is at our house, off street, down the driveway. Neighborhood parking should be ample.

Lodging

There aren't any hotels near our house, but there's a lot of AirBNBs and VRBOs. We strongly recommend you book sooner rather than later if that's your intention, as it will be Seafair weeknd and it could get picked over quick.

The party will go all night, but we have a fairly expansive yard with plenty of camping room.

Travel

If you're flying you'll want to fly into SeaTac.

You can take the Link light rail train from the airport or from the north, but the closest stop is Othello station, a little over a mile from our house. The closest bus is the 7, which stops about a half a mile from our house at Rainier Ave S & S Holly St

Eat and Drank

Bar Del Corso - Woodfired pizza, exceptional Italian small plates, tasty cocktails. Our favorite restaurant

La Medusa - Fancy pasta. Very good, a little more upscale

Star Brass - Great burgers, our go-to bar

Lottie's - No frills neighborhood bar. Great karaoke on Tuesdays
